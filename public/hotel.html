Activities while in Zanzibar

Your Gateway to Exotic Island Adventures

Jozani Forest

Jozani Forest is Zanzibar’s only national park, home to rare red colobus monkeys, lush greenery, and fascinating wildlife walking trails.

Mnemba Atoll

Mnemba Atoll is a stunning marine reserve known for excellent diving and snorkeling with colorful coral reefs and abundant marine life.

Nungwi Beach

Nungwi Beach features pristine white sands and crystal-clear waters, perfect for swimming, snorkeling, and enjoying vibrant nightlife along Zanzibar’s northern coast.

Water Sports

Zanzibar offers unforgettable aquatic adventures — glide over turquoise waters in a glass kayak, swim with dolphins or sea turtles, or walk beneath the waves in a magical undersea experience. Whether you seek thrill or tranquility, the island’s warm, crystal-clear ocean is the perfect playground for all water lovers.

Prison Island (Changuu Island)

Prison Island is famous for giant Aldabra tortoises and turquoise waters, offering snorkeling, history, and beautiful beaches just off Stone Town.

Spice Farms

Zanzibar’s Spice Farms offer guided tours through fragrant plantations, showcasing cloves, nutmeg, cinnamon, and other spices that shaped the island’s history.
